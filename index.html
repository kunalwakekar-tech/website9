The Reality of the Current Market

Objective data for the retiring business owner.

The Small-to-Medium Business (SMB) market is currently experiencing a historic volume of transitions. However, statistical evidence remains consistent: only a minority of companies listed for sale reach a successful close. This failure rate is typically the result of inadequate financial preparation, improper market positioning, or a breach in operational confidentiality.

Our role as advisors is to mitigate these risks. We provide a bridge between your current operational state and a successful hand-off to a qualified successor.

Financial Recasting

We analyze your historical financials to identify discretionary expenses and add-backs. This process ensures that your "Seller's Discretionary Earnings" (SDE) are accurately represented to potential buyers.

Confidential Marketing

We utilize "Blind Business Profiles" to solicit interest without revealing your company's identity. This protects your relationships with employees, customers, and vendors during the process.

Buyer Qualification

Every prospective buyer is vetted for financial liquidity and operational experience. We ensure that only high-intent, qualified individuals or groups receive sensitive information.

The Advisory Process

A step-by-step approach to a successful exit.

1. Assessment: We begin with a deep-dive audit of your financials and operations to determine a defensible market range for your business.

2. Documentation: We prepare a Comprehensive Information Memorandum (CIM) that details your company's strengths, opportunities, and financial history.

3. Vetting & Negotiation: We manage the inquiry process, oversee NDAs, and lead negotiations to secure a Letter of Intent (LOI) that reflects your goals.

4. Due Diligence & Closing: We coordinate with your legal and tax professionals to manage the buyer's audit and drive the transaction to a final wire transfer.
