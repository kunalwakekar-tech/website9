The Advisory Process

A structured process designed to move from inquiry to close.

1. Assessment: We begin with a deep-dive review of your financials and operations to determine a defensible market range for your business.

2. Documentation: We prepare a comprehensive information package that details your company’s strengths, opportunities, and financial history.

3. Confidential Data Room: We host and organize a confidential digital data room under NDA to securely exchange deal documents and financials with interested parties.

4. Vetting & Negotiation: We manage the inquiry process, oversee NDAs, and lead negotiations to secure a letter of intent that reflects your goals.

5. Due Diligence & Closing: We coordinate with your legal and tax professionals to manage the buyer’s diligence process and drive the transaction toward closing.
